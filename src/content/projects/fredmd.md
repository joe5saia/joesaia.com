FredMD provides tools for working with the FRED-MD dataset, a widely-used macroeconomic dataset maintained by the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis.

The package implements factor estimation methods and information criteria to help researchers determine the optimal number of factors for forecasting and analysis.
